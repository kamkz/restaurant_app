Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Butare Town, Huye District, Rwanda, Butare Flavors brings you the authentic taste of Rwanda with a contemporary presentation. Our commitment to fresh, local ingredients and traditional cooking methods creates an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates Rwandan hospitality.

About Butare Flavors

Located in the vibrant Butare Town of Huye District, Rwanda, Butare Flavors has been serving authentic Rwandan cuisine since our founding. We pride ourselves on sourcing the finest local ingredients and supporting our community farmers.

Our commitment extends beyond great food - we're dedicated to preserving Rwandan culinary traditions while embracing modern cooking techniques to create memorable dining experiences.

Our Journey
